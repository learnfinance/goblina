## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Prompt Editing

### Camera Work:
- `"Static wide shot"` - No movement, shows full scene
- `"Push-in from medium to close-up"` - Dramatic zoom
- `"Handheld, shaky camera"` - Energetic feel
- `"Smooth tracking shot"` - Professional look
- `"Dutch angle"` - Tilted for tension

### Lighting:
- `"Soft fluorescent office lighting"` - Realistic office
- `"Dramatic rim lighting"` - Silhouette effect
- `"Golden hour warm lighting"` - Romantic/nostalgic
- `"Neon lighting, cyberpunk"` - Stylized look
- `"Harsh shadows, film noir"` - Dramatic mood

### Character Details:
- `"Close-up of eyes widening"` - Emphasize reaction
- `"Over-the-shoulder shot"` - POV perspective
- `"Character turns to camera, breaks 4th wall"` - Direct engagement
- `"Exaggerated cartoon expression"` - More comedic

### Motion:
- `"Slow motion"` - Dramatic emphasis
- `"Fast-paced, snappy cuts"` - Energetic
- `"Character leaps dramatically"` - Action
- `"Subtle head tilt and smirk"` - Personality
